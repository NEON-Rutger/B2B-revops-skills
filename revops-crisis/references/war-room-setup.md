# War Room Setup & Operations Guide
**For: B2B scale-ups €15M–150M ARR**

Operational checklist for activating and running an effective crisis war room.

---

## WAR ROOM TRIGGER CRITERIA

**Activate war room when:**
- Pipeline coverage drops below 2x coverage (Q at risk)
- Forecast miss detected mid-quarter (>10% gap to guidance)
- Revenue-critical system fails (CRM outage, forecasting system down)
- Major customer churn event (>€250k/mo at risk)
- Rep attrition crisis (>3 reps departing in 30 days)
- Board-level notification required

**Do NOT activate if:**
- Single deal is lost (handle in regular weekly forecast)
- Routine month-end reconciliation issues
- Low-impact data quality issue (<€50k affected)

---

## PHYSICAL/VIRTUAL WAR ROOM SETUP

### Core Team (Non-Negotiable)
- **CRO or VP Sales** — Decision authority; crisis owner
- **RevOps Lead** — Data, analytics, reporting
- **Finance/CFO** — Revenue verification, board communication
- **Sales Manager(s)** — Territory intel, rep support
- **Marketing Lead** (if pipeline-driven) — Demand gen support options

### Extended Team (Add as Needed)
- **Product Lead** (if churn/data crisis) — Customer needs, roadmap
- **CS Lead** (if churn crisis) — Customer health, retention strategies
- **IT/Systems Admin** (if data/system crisis) — Data integrity, backups
- **CEO** (if forecast miss) — Board strategy, capital decisions

### Physical War Room Setup
- **Location:** Dedicated space with large displays, whiteboard
- **Duration:** 1–2 week daily standups; then 3x weekly
- **Technology:**
  - Laptop/desktop per core team member
  - Large screen(s) showing live dashboards
  - Whiteboard for playbook tracking
  - Phone/video for remote participants
  - Shared spreadsheet accessible to all (live deal tracker)

### Virtual War Room Setup (If Remote)
- **Dedicated Slack channel:** #crisis-warroom-[crisis-type]
- **Daily standup:** Scheduled same time daily (e.g., 8 AM CET)
- **Shared documents:** Google Sheet with live deal pipeline tracker, metrics dashboard
- **Video: 30 min daily standup, cameras on**
- **Tools:** CRM open on shared screen; live metric updates
- **Async updates:** Slack thread with 6 PM CET end-of-day update

---

## DAILY STANDUP STRUCTURE

**Duration:** 15 minutes (strict)

**Attendees:** Core team only; optional observers on mute

**Agenda (Fixed Order):**
1. **Metric update** (2 min): Pipeline coverage | Pipeline value | Forecast variance | Churn status
   - Green/yellow/red status for each metric
2. **Deal updates** (5 min): Top 10 deals by revenue impact
   - Status change since yesterday? Probability shift?
   - Anything blocking that war room can unblock?
3. **Action items** (5 min): What changed since yesterday?
   - Who owns what by EOD today?
   - Any escalations needed?
   - Wins to celebrate?
4. **Forecast revision** (2 min): New forecast based on last 24 hours
   - Did we move the needle?

**Standup artifacts:**
- One-page metric snapshot (emailed 6 PM previous day)
- Deal tracker updated by 8 AM before standup
- Action item log tracked in Slack/spreadsheet

**Discipline rules:**
- Start and end on time
- No long discussions (take to side conversation)
- Decisions made by CRO in real-time; no "let me check and get back"
- Decision log updated immediately (Slack thread)

## METRICS TO TRACK DURING CRISIS

### Hourly (First 48 Hours)
- **Pipeline coverage:** Current vs. 2x benchmark
- **Top 20 deal status:** Green/yellow/red per deal
- **Forecast variance:** Current forecast vs. guidance
- **Blockers identified:** Number of deals stuck; by reason

### Daily (Weeks 1–2)
- **Pipeline coverage:** Movement toward 2x
- **New pipeline generated:** Outbound meetings set, partner pipeline activated
- **Deal acceleration:** Deals moved up in timeline
- **Forecast confidence:** Forecast variance to guidance
- **Action item completion:** % of previous day's actions done
- **Churn rate** (if churn crisis): New churns identified; save plays activated

### Weekly (Weeks 2+)
- **Pipeline recovery trajectory:** On track to 2x by target date?
- **Demand gen output:** Inbound meetings scheduled; quality
- **Rep activity:** Calls, meetings, proposals by rep (if attrition)
- **Customer retention:** Save play outcomes; win-back results
- **Board narrative:** What we'll tell CEO/board this week

### Dashboard Display
- **War room screen:** Live Salesforce/reporting dashboard
  - Pipeline coverage chart
  - Top 50 deals with status, probability, value, close date
  - Forecast variance to goal
  - Weekly inbound meeting count
- **Physical board (if in-person):** Playbook checklist with checkmarks
  - Week 1: [ ] Deal acceleration [ ] Outbound blitz [ ] Partner activation
  - Week 2: [ ] Demand gen running [ ] Rep support [ ] Rep hiring started

## ESCALATION PATHS

### Daily Escalation (During Standup)
- **CRO makes decision on:** Deal-level authority, rep compensation, pricing discounts
- **CFO decides:** Forecast disclosure, revenue recognition, contingency planning
- **Marketing lead decides:** Ad spend increase, campaign timing

### CEO Escalation (When to Involve)
- **Forecast miss** (>5% of annual guidance): Brief CEO immediately; daily updates
- **Major customer churn** (>€500k): Executive-level save play required
- **Board notification required:** CEO + CFO decide timing and disclosure
- **System/data crisis** (data integrity loss): CEO briefed same day; stakeholder comms

### Board Escalation (When to Notify)
- **Missed guidance:** CFO notifies board chair; structured update in next call
- **Forecast revision** (>10% down): Included in next earnings call or special notice
- **Timeline:** If crisis detected mid-month, notify board by week 1 if miss likely

### External Escalation
- **Investor/board relations:** CFO leads; RevOps provides data
- **Customer communication:** CEO/CRO determines if board-level customers need heads-up
- **Financing impact:** CFO assesses if revenue miss triggers covenant questions

---

## CRISIS WAR ROOM EXIT CRITERIA

**War room transitions to "watch mode" when:**
- Pipeline coverage recovers to 2x+ (sustained for 3 days)
- Forecast variance closes to <5% of goal
- All Tier 1 deals have clear status (closed, delayed to next quarter)
- Daily standup no longer needed (shift to 3x weekly, then weekly)

**War room is formally closed when:**
- Full month of normalized operations without crisis triggers
- All playbook actions completed or embedded into normal process
- Post-crisis retrospective completed and shared
