# Compensation Plan Templates
## B2B SaaS (€15M-€150M ARR) - European Market

---

## 1. SDR/BDR Compensation Plan

**Role:** Sales Development Representative / Business Development Representative

**OTE Range:** €30,000 - €42,000 (varies by experience, geography, segment)

**Structure:**
- Base Salary: 70% (€21,000 - €29,400)
- Variable/Commission: 30% (€9,000 - €12,600)

**Key Metrics & Quotas:**
- Monthly qualified meetings booked: 8-12 per SDR (varies by segment)
- SQLs (Sales Qualified Leads) per month: 15-25 depending on vertical
- Response time to inbound: <2 hours
- Demo attendance rate: 60% minimum

**Commission Structure:**
- €85-€120 per meeting booked (€200+ value deals only)
- €45-€65 per SQL delivered
- Monthly bonus pool if team hits 100% of meetings target: +10-15% of variable pay
- Accelerator at 120% of target: 1.5x commission multiplier

**Ramp Period:**
- Months 1-2: 50% quota (4-6 meetings/week expected)
- Months 3-4: 75% quota
- Month 5+: 100% quota (full OTE achievable)
- New hire draw: €2,000-€3,000/month during ramp

**Benefits & Bonuses:**
- Quarterly attainment bonus (team-based)
- SPIFs (Special Performance Incentive Funds) for product launches
- Leaderboard incentives (€500-€1,000 monthly for top performer)

## 2. AE (SMB - €5K-€20K ACV) Compensation Plan

**Role:** Account Executive - Small Business Segment

**OTE Range:** €45,000 - €65,000 (city-dependent: Amsterdam +10%, regional -5%)

**Structure:**
- Base Salary: 60% (€27,000 - €39,000)
- Variable/Commission: 40% (€18,000 - €26,000)

**Key Metrics & Quotas:**
- Annual revenue quota: €300,000 - €400,000 per AE
- Deal count: 15-20 deals/year (avg deal €18-€25K ACV)
- Win rate: 25-30% expected
- Sales cycle: 45-60 days average

**Commission Structure:**
- Commission rate: 5-6% of first-year ACV
- Example: €20K deal = €1,000-€1,200 commission
- Accelerator at 120% of annual quota: 1.25x multiplier
- Decelerator at <80% of quota: 0.75x multiplier

**Quota-to-OTE Ratio:** 5.5:1 to 6:1 (€400K quota generates ~€30-36K variable at 100%)

**Ramp Period:**
- Months 1-3: 40% quota + €1,500/month draw
- Months 4-6: 60% quota + €1,000/month draw
- Months 7-9: 80% quota
- Month 10+: 100% quota (full OTE achievable by year-end)

**Benefits & Bonuses:**
- Quarterly SPIFs (new product, named account wins)
- Upsell bonus: 3% commission on expansion deals
- Team attainment bonus: +8% if team hits 110% of target
- Annual President's Club (top 10%): €2,000-€5,000 + trip

## 3. AE (Mid-Market - €25K-€75K ACV) Compensation Plan

**Role:** Account Executive - Mid-Market Segment

**OTE Range:** €65,000 - €95,000 (city-dependent: Amsterdam +12%, regional -5%)

**Structure:**
- Base Salary: 55% (€35,750 - €52,250)
- Variable/Commission: 45% (€29,250 - €42,750)

**Key Metrics & Quotas:**
- Annual revenue quota: €500,000 - €650,000 per AE
- Deal count: 8-12 deals/year (avg deal €50-€65K ACV)
- Win rate: 28-35% expected
- Sales cycle: 75-120 days average

**Commission Structure:**
- Commission rate: 5-5.5% of first-year ACV
- Example: €60K deal = €3,000-€3,300 commission
- Accelerator at 120% of quota: 1.35x multiplier (higher leverage)
- Decelerator at <75% of quota: 0.75x multiplier
- Multi-year deals: additional 0.5% for commitment >2 years

**Quota-to-OTE Ratio:** 5.5:1 to 6:1 (€575K quota generates ~€38-45K variable at 100%)

**Ramp Period:**
- Months 1-4: 35% quota + €2,000/month draw
- Months 5-8: 60% quota + €1,500/month draw
- Months 9-12: 85% quota
- Month 13+: 100% quota (full OTE achievable by month 12-13)

**Benefits & Bonuses:**
- Quarterly SPIFs: new verticals, customer success stories
- Upsell bonus: 4% commission on expansion deals to existing accounts
- Team attainment bonus: +10% if team achieves 110%+ of target
- President's Club (top 15%): €3,000-€7,500 + trip

## 4. AE (Enterprise - €100K+ ACV) Compensation Plan

**Role:** Account Executive - Enterprise Segment

**OTE Range:** €100,000 - €150,000 (city-dependent: Amsterdam +15%, regional +5%)

**Structure:**
- Base Salary: 50% (€50,000 - €75,000)
- Variable/Commission: 50% (€50,000 - €75,000)

**Key Metrics & Quotas:**
- Annual revenue quota: €1,000,000 - €1,500,000 per AE
- Deal count: 5-8 deals/year (avg deal €150-€250K ACV)
- Win rate: 30-40% expected
- Sales cycle: 120-180 days average

**Commission Structure:**
- Commission rate: 4.5-5% of first-year ACV
- Example: €200K deal = €9,000-€10,000 commission
- Accelerator at 120% of quota: 1.5x multiplier (highest leverage)
- Decelerator at <70% of quota: 0.7x multiplier
- Multi-year deals: +0.25% per additional year (3-year = +0.75%)
- Expansion quota (existing accounts): weighted at 6% commission

**Quota-to-OTE Ratio:** 5.5:1 to 6:1 (€1.2M quota generates ~€60-75K variable at 100%)

**Ramp Period:**
- Months 1-6: 25% quota + €3,000/month draw
- Months 7-12: 50% quota + €2,000/month draw
- Months 13-18: 75% quota
- Month 19+: 100% quota (full OTE achievable by month 18-20)

**Benefits & Bonuses:**
- Quarterly SPIFs: strategic accounts, reference customers, multi-year deals
- Account retention bonus: 2% commission if renewal rate >95% on book
- Team attainment bonus: +12% if team hits 110%+ of target
- President's Club (top 10%): €5,000-€10,000 + trip + equity
- Annual mid-year review for comp plan adjustment based on market

## 5. Customer Success Manager (CSM) Compensation Plan

**Role:** Customer Success Manager (Onboarding & Growth)

**OTE Range:** €42,000 - €58,000 (varies by segment managed, geography)

**Structure:**
- Base Salary: 75% (€31,500 - €43,500)
- Variable/Commission: 25% (€10,500 - €14,500)

**Key Metrics & Quotas:**
- NRR (Net Revenue Retention) target: 105-115% assigned segment
- Customer health score: 90%+ in Green/Yellow (max 10% Red)
- Expansion MRR: €15,000-€25,000/quarter per CSM
- CSAT score: 8.0+ (0-10 scale)
- Churn rate: <5% annually on book

**Commission Structure:**
- NRR bonus: +€1,500 if team NRR >110% annually
- Expansion commission: 3% on incremental expansion revenue delivered
- Upsell bonus: €500-€1,000 per successfully closed expansion deal
- Health score bonus: +€800/quarter if >95% Green + Yellow accounts
- Churn prevention bonus: €400 per at-risk account successfully saved

**Ramp Period:**
- Months 1-3: Onboarding phase (assigned team of 3-5 accounts)
- Months 4-6: Growing to 8-10 accounts, some commission at 50%
- Months 7-12: Full book of 12-15 accounts, 100% commission

**Benefits & Bonuses:**
- Quarterly attainment bonus: +8% if personal NRR >110%
- Annual Customer Champion award: €1,000-€2,000 + recognition
- Peer recognition bonus pool: €500/month distributed by peers

## 6. Sales Manager / Team Lead Compensation Plan

**Role:** Sales Manager (manages 4-6 AEs)

**OTE Range:** €70,000 - €105,000 (varies by team segment)

**Structure:**
- Base Salary: 65% (€45,500 - €68,250)
- Variable/Commission: 35% (€24,500 - €36,750)

**Key Metrics & Quotas:**
- Team quota: €2,500,000 - €3,500,000 (aggregate team target)
- Individual close quota (required to remain hands-on): 20% of team quota
- Pipeline coverage ratio: 4:1 minimum (€4 pipeline per €1 quota)
- Win rate (team): 25-30% maintained
- AE turnover: <15% annually

**Commission Structure:**
- Team attainment commission: 8% on dollars over 100% of team quota
- Personal deal commission: 3% on personal closes (reduced rate vs AE)
- Team override: 1% of all team revenue (regardless of personal attainment)
- Quarterly bonuses tied to:
  - Team quota attainment (40% weight)
  - Deal quality/win rate (30% weight)
  - Pipeline development (20% weight)
  - Team retention (10% weight)

**Quota-to-OTE Ratio:** 4.5:1 to 5.5:1 (€1.5-1.6M team quota generates ~€30-35K variable at 100%)

**Benefits & Bonuses:**
- Annual discretionary bonus pool: €5,000-€10,000 based on business results
- Management training allowance: €1,500/year
- President's Club (top managers): €3,000-€8,000 + trip
- Equity consideration for long-term retention

## 7. VP Sales / Chief Revenue Officer Compensation Plan

**Role:** VP Sales or Chief Revenue Officer

**OTE Range:** €130,000 - €220,000 (depends on company stage, market)

**Structure:**
- Base Salary: 55-60% (€71,500 - €132,000)
- Cash Variable/Bonus: 25-30% (€32,500 - €66,000)
- Equity: 0.2% - 0.8% (depending on company stage & funding)

**Key Metrics & Quotas:**
- Revenue quota: €5,000,000 - €15,000,000+ (entire sales organization)
- Company-wide revenue targets aligned with corporate OKRs
- Pipeline-to-quota coverage: 4:1 minimum maintained
- Quota-to-plan accuracy: ±5% variance accepted
- Sales organization health: AE retention >85%, quota attainment >90%

**Bonus Structure (typically paid quarterly/annually):**
- Revenue attainment: 50% weight
  - 100% of target = 100% bonus earned
  - 110%+ = 1.5x bonus multiplier
  - <80% = 0.5x bonus multiplier
- Strategic MBOs: 50% weight (set quarterly)
  - Sales process improvements
  - Market expansion / new vertical launch
  - Sales team development / bench building
  - Customer retention / NRR targets

**Example:** €130K OTE (€65K base + €65K bonus)
- At 100% revenue quota + 100% MBO: €65K bonus earned
- At 110% revenue quota + 125% MBO: €130K bonus earned (1.5x)

**Ramp Period:**
- Month 1-3: Onboarding & assessment phase, 100% base paid
- Month 4+: Full variable kicks in based on quarterly attainment
- Annual reset of targets at fiscal year start

**Equity & Long-term:**
- 0.3-0.5% equity grant typical for growth stage (€15M-€100M ARR)
- 4-year vest, 1-year cliff standard
- Equity reviews annually based on company valuation & role expansion

**Benefits & Bonuses:**
- Annual discretionary bonus pool: €10,000-€30,000+ for business results exceeding targets
- Executive healthcare package (premium)
- Car allowance: €800-€1,200/month or actual car provision
- Conference/training budget: €5,000-€10,000 annually
- Deferred compensation / retention bonus if applicable

## Notes on Implementation

**Commission & Ramp Pacing:**
- All commissions paid on invoice or cash received (industry standard)
- Ramp schedules can flex based on candidate experience (experienced hires ramp faster)
- Draw advances recovered through future commissions or forfeited on separation

**Quota Setting:**
- Bottom-up quota setting recommended (territory potential × achievement factor)
- Annual quota set by Q4, communicated by Dec 15 for new fiscal year
- Mid-year adjustments allowed (+/- 10%) only if market conditions warrant

**Regional Adjustments (Multiplicative):**
- Amsterdam / Utrecht: Base +10-12%
- DACH region (Germany): Base +5-8%
- UK: Base +3-5%
- Nordics: Base +5-10%
- Rest of NL: Base -3% to -5%

**Plan Reviews:**
- Annual compensation review: Q4 (October-November)
- Market benchmarking: yearly against Radford, Mercer, or custom surveys
- Individual adjustments: merit-based, no more than +8-10% annually to avoid compression
