# Time-to-Value: The Retention Foundation

On-demand reference for the expansion-revenue-architect skill.

TTV is the most underrated lever for both GRR and expansion readiness. Customers who find value quickly retain better AND expand more.

## TTV-to-Retention Correlation (Sourced)

| TTV Achievement | Impact | Source |
|----------------|--------|--------|
| Value within 14 days | **80%+ retention at month 12**; vs. 35-50% retention if value delayed beyond 30 days | SaaS Onboarding Statistics 2026 |
| No engagement within first 3 days | **90% churn probability** | Digital Applied 2026 |
| 7-day activation benchmark | **69% correlation** with 3-month retention; represents top-quartile activation performance | Amplitude 2025 |
| No value milestone within 14 days | **98% churn within two weeks** | Amplitude 2025 (2,600+ B2B SaaS companies) |
| Personalized vs. generic onboarding | **40% higher retention**; tailored paths improve Day-30 retention by 52% | SaaS Onboarding Statistics 2026 |
| SaaS average TTV | **1 day, 12 hours** (median); under 1 hour = strong self-serve; under 5 minutes = world-class | Digital Applied 2026 |
| Early churn window | **60-70% of annual churn** occurs in first 90 days; **40-60%** of users churn in first 30 days (never experience promised value) | SaaS Onboarding Statistics 2026 |

## TTV Benchmarks by Segment

| Segment | Good | Great | Best-in-Class | Source |
|---------|------|-------|---------------|--------|
| SMB | <14 days | <7 days | <3 days | CS Ops Reference |
| Mid-Market | <60 days | <30 days | <14 days | CS Ops Reference |

**Implication for expansion:** Fix TTV first, then build expansion. Customers who haven't reached value will never expand. **43% of SMB customer losses occur in the first 90 days** (Chameleon 2024), the onboarding window is the make-or-break moment for the entire right side of the bowtie.

## Data Source Notes

Data refreshed 2026-07-15 with 2025-2026 benchmarks. Earlier Amplitude 2024 case-study claims (e.g. 183% churn reduction for 24-hour value attainment) could not be independently verified against 2026 sources and have been replaced with segmented retention thresholds from current research. The 7-day activation and 3-month retention correlation remains stable across both vintage datasets.
